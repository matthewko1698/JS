Happiness and Self Image Satisfaction

There are a lot of attributes to life that contribute to happiness, but in today's modern world where technology is new and changing society, our image has become more and more important to how we guage self worth. Social media and the entertainment industry have lots of power in the types of people and actions are heralded as ideal.

Dataset 1: Happiness Report

The first dataset that is available is the world happiness report. This data gives a comprehensive happiness score along with contributers to this happiness score, such as economy, family, generosity, and trust.

These specific datasets may not comprehensively reveal the truth behind many of the issues surrounding self image and happiness or lack of happiness but I hope that it provides a good start. The happiness survey can help us determine what countries are happier than others and through additional data we can hope to understand why. Being overweight and obese is a significant part of a person's image and if people are not comfortable with that image, people may try to avoid looking this way. Suicide rates tell an end story of unhappiness and because there are many sources of unhappiness, this data would need to be supplemented with additional data but I believe it is a good starting point especially in determining if certain countries are abnormal compared to others and why. Hopefully the data can help us understand how fixation on one's image can relate to happiness.
